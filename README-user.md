# Veya

**Get paid in Bitcoin. Run your business like a pro.**

Veya is the merchant operating system for the Bitcoin economy. Create invoices, receive stablecoin payments on Bitcoin, and manage your entire business from one dashboard,  while your customers experience a payment flow as clean and instant as anything they've used before.


## What Veya Does

Veya connects merchants to the RGB protocol, a breakthrough technology that brings real stablecoins to Bitcoin. That means you get the speed and finality of Bitcoin with the price stability of the US dollar, no volatility, no card networks issues and also no banks sitting between you and your money.

Here's the full flow:

1. You create an invoice in seconds
2. Veya generates a unique payment link
3. You send the link to your customer
4. They pay with an RGB-compatible Bitcoin wallet
5. Your dashboard confirms the moment it settles


## Getting Started

### Step 1 — Create your account

Go to the Veya signup page and enter:
- Your name
- Your email
- A password
- Your store name

Your store is created instantly. You land straight in your dashboard.


### Step 2 — Get familiar with your dashboard

Your dashboard has eight sections:

| Section | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Overview** | Your numbers at a glance — total revenue, invoices paid, pending payments, total customers |
| **Invoices** | Create new invoices, see all past invoices, filter by status |
| **Customers** | Everyone who has ever paid you — how many times, how much in total |
| **Payment Portal** | Preview what your customers see when they receive a payment link |
| **Staff** | Invite team members and control what they can access |
| **Support** | Message Veya support directly — replies come in real time |
| **Notifications** | Alerts when an invoice is paid or a support message arrives |
| **Settings** | Your store details and account preferences |


### Step 3 — Create your first invoice

1. Click **Invoices** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Invoice**
3. Enter the amount and the customer's name
4. Click **Create**

Veya generates a unique payment link — something like `veya.com/pay/INV-00001`.

Send it to your customer however works for you — WhatsApp, email, SMS, wherever.


### Step 4 — Your customer pays

Your customer opens the link. They see:

- The amount due
- A QR code and a payment address
- A countdown timer showing how long the invoice is valid

They open their RGB-compatible Bitcoin wallet, scan the QR code, and send the payment.

The page updates automatically. It moves through three states:

```
Awaiting payment  →  Confirming  →  Confirmed ✓
```

When it hits Confirmed, your dashboard updates instantly.


### Step 5 — Track everything

Every payment is saved automatically. Go to **Customers** to see everyone who has paid you — Veya tracks their name, how many invoices they've had, and their total spend.

Go to **Overview** to see your running totals.


## Managing Your Team

Go to **Staff** and invite team members. There are three roles:

| Role | What they can do |
|---|---|
| **Owner** | Full access to everything |
| **Manager** | Can create invoices, view customers and revenue |
| **Cashier** | Can create invoices only — no access to revenue data |

Invite a cashier if you want staff to be able to create payment links without seeing your financials.


## Invoice Statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Pending** | Created, waiting for payment |
| **Paid** | Payment confirmed on Bitcoin |
| **Expired** | Invoice window closed before payment was received |


## What Wallet Does My Customer Need?

Veya processes payments over the RGB protocol. Your customers need an RGB-compatible wallet to pay. Currently supported:

- **UTEXO Wallet**
- **Tribe Wallet**

Both are available on iOS and Android. If your customer doesn't have one, direct them to download TRIBE Wallet — it's the simplest option.


## Support

Go to the **Support** section in your dashboard and open a message thread. Veya's team will reply directly — you'll get a notification the moment they respond.

## FAQ

**Do I need a Bitcoin wallet to use Veya?**
No. Veya handles the payment infrastructure. Your customers need a wallet to pay you — you just need a Veya account.

**What currency do I invoice in?**
USDT issued on Bitcoin via the RGB protocol. This is a stablecoin — it tracks the US dollar, so you're not exposed to Bitcoin price volatility.

**What happens if an invoice expires before the customer pays?**
The invoice is marked Expired. You can create a new one for the same customer in seconds.


**Can I have multiple stores?**
Each Veya account has one store. Multi-store support is on the roadmap.
